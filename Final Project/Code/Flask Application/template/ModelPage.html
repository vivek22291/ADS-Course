What is the YELP DATA CHALLENGE 2016?

Yelp has given it's data for Users, Reviews provided by the Users, the tip and check in information. The data provided is specific to a business posted on Yelp. The objective of the challenge is to get insights on the data and provide a mechanism for Yelp to interpret the data.

Data composition

Yelp has provided their data in the form of JSON files that we have extracted to CSV format. The cities included are: Cities: U.K.: Edinburgh Germany: Karlsruhe Canada: Montreal and Waterloo U.S.: Pittsburgh, Charlotte, Urbana-Champaign, Phoenix, Las Vegas, Madison We have restricted the scope of our project to only the U.S cities
